# Getting Started with Loopletter

Welcome to Loopletter! This guide will help you set up your account and send your first email campaign in minutes.

## What is Loopletter?

Loopletter is an email marketing platform built specifically for artists, musicians, and creators. We help you:

- Build and manage your fan base
- Create beautiful email campaigns
- Track engagement and analytics
- Automate your email marketing
- Ensure high deliverability rates

## Quick Start Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Create Your Account
    Sign up at [loopletter.com](https://loopletter.com) and complete the onboarding process.
  </Step>
  
  <Step>
    ### Set Up Your Profile
    Add your artist name, bio, and branding to personalize your emails.
  </Step>
  
  <Step>
    ### Import Your First Fans
    Upload a CSV file or manually add email addresses to start building your audience.
  </Step>
  
  <Step>
    ### Verify Your Domain
    Set up email authentication to improve deliverability and build trust.
  </Step>
  
  <Step>
    ### Send Your First Campaign
    Create and send your first email to welcome your fans.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Features Overview

### Campaign Management
Create professional email campaigns with our visual editor, schedule sends, and track performance in real-time.

### Audience Segmentation
Organize your fans into targeted segments based on location, engagement, tags, and custom criteria.

### Analytics Dashboard
Monitor open rates, click rates, subscriber growth, and campaign performance with detailed analytics.

### Team Collaboration
Invite team members with different permission levels to help manage your email marketing.
